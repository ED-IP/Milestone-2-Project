What Toronto can offer to you

You are about to enter in one of the most multicultural cities in the world. Welcome to a place where art and culture from all the corners of the globe joint togheter to create a unique experience. Here we show you just an small part of what you can find when visting us.

Welcome to Toronto!




Outdors

Enjoy the multiple open spaces and parks that we can offer you, like the Rouge National Urban Park, largest urban park in North America or take a little trip on a ferry to visit the Toronto Islands. And if you want something different you can also relax playing a game of golf in one of the several public courses.

Multiculture

Explore a city with a broad multicultural influence. From Chinatown to Roncesvalles neighbourhood, you would be able to find people from India, Italy, Korea... Just get lost in our diverse city and find your favourite place... if you can just choose one!


Transport

Move inside the city with confidence thanks to a top of the line transport network. Featuring three heavy-rail rapid transit lines the Toronto subway system will allow you to go to your destination in time. If the subway is not for you we also have an extensive network of buses, streetcars and even bicycle lanes.
